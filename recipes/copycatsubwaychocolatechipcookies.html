Copycat Subway Chocolate Chip Cookies

A picture of the delicious chocolate chip cookies

This is a recipe I found online created by aqueenathekitchen. Her recipe will give the most delicious chocolate chip cookies, that taste just like Subways...maybe even better!

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup of unsalted butter/margarine
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1.25 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp table salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips

    Steps

  1. Melt the butter in the microwave, don't melt if using margarine
  2. In a bowl/mixer bowl, add the butter, sugars, and vanilla
  3. Add in the eggs
  4. Add in the salt, baking powder, and baking soda
  5. Put rice in 8" pan
  6. Add in the flour, 1/2 cup at a time, mixing after each addition
  7. Mix in the chocolate chips
  8. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper
  9. Scoop dough into 24 equal sized balls
  10. Chill the dough for 1 hour
  11. Heat oven to 350 deg F
  12. Bake dough for 11-13 mins until edges are just starting to brown
  13. Cool for a few mins on pan before moving to a wire rack to cool completely
