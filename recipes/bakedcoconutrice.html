Baked Coconut Rice

A bowl of baked coconut rice

This is a recipe I found online created by Nagi Maehashi. Her recipe will give you rice that is not gluey or sticky, but rather light and fluffy with a hint of coconut.

    Ingredients

  • 2 cups jasmine rice
  • 14 oz coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 5 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt

    Steps

  1. Rinse rice until water is clear
  2. Soak rice for an hour
  3. Drain the rice and leave in colainder for 5 min
  4. Preheat oven to 400 deg F
  5. Put rice in 8" pan
  6. Heat coconut milk, water, sugar, and salt in saucepan to a boil until sugar dissolves
  7. Pour hot coconut milk mixture over the rice in pan
  8. Quickly cover pan with tin foil
  9. Place pan in over and bake for 40 mins
  10. Rest the rice for 15 min with foil still covering pan
  11. Fluff rice with spatula and the serve
